About Factor Three Software

Located in sunny Scottsdale, Arizona, Factor Three Software has been creating custom software applications for 10 years. We specialize in cross platform mobile apps. That means, our apps run on iOS, Android and Windows, sharing a large percentage of code among platforms. What that means to our customers is a much, much lower cost to have their apps run on more than one operating system. Our apps are completely native (not web views) which enables us to take full advantage of all of the devices features.
